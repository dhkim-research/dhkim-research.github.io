About Me

I am a researcher at ETH Zürich’s Chair of Architecture and Building Systems, studying how light, material, and adaptive façades influence visual and thermal comfort in buildings. My work combines field measurements, HDR imaging, and human-centred environmental modelling.

Previously, I was part of the LIPID Lab (EPFL) and Indoor Environment Group (TU Delft), focusing on the integration of daylight, health, and occupant-centric design.
